Why June 1st

A local moment, framed with care.

June 1st is Children’s Day in Romania and in several European countries. While the United Nations marks World Children’s Day on November 20, we are using this June 1st moment to celebrate the childlike curiosity behind every civic voice.

It is an invitation to remember the curiosity behind every civic voice: the child who asked why, imagined differently, and believed that better worlds could be built.
